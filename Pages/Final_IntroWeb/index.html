About

My name is Haven Hamelin, and I am attending IUPUI for Game Design. I have a passion for creative endeavors in the digital space, and have been coding since a young age. Some particular skills of mine would be my knowledge of many programming languages, as well as a blooming skill set in digital artwork (namely pixel art), and a novice understanding of music creation. Some examples of programming languages I am proficient in are Java, Javascript, Python, and GDscript (for the Godot game engine). I would say I have a very dedicated personality; when something interests me I don't let it go. To go along with this, I also heavily value the spending of time developing one's passions. I feel as though the world likes to discourage people from dedicating their lives to doing what they love, but I don't agree with that sentiment.
